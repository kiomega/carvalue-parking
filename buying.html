Buying a new or used car

Buying a new or used car is always an exciting moment. Whether you’re getting your first car, or upgrading to something more modern, it pays to do your research.

Before you jump on the first car you see, there’s a few things you should do to ensure you’re getting a good deal.

First Steps

The first step when looking at new or used cars is to decide how you’re going to fund the purchase and how much you’re willing to spend. This will affect all future decisions you make.

Find out more in First Steps for Buyers

Car Valuation

Once you’ve got a rough idea on what sort of car you’d like to buy, you’ll need to work out if what the seller is asking for is accurate. This can help you know how much you can negotiate if you decide to take the car for a spin.

Find out more in Car Valuation for Buyers
