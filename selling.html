Selling a used car

Selling a car can be exciting moment. You get to pass on your pre-loved vehicle to someone else, and (hopefully) get the full value of your car in cash.

But before you sell your car, there’s a few things you should do to ensure you get as much money for it as possible.

Car Valuation

The first step is to find out an estimate of what your car is worth. This will guide you in future decisions as you’ll have a rough idea of how much you can get for your car.

Remember: the value of your car is the amount of money when a buyer and a seller mutually agree on a price. No more, no less.

Every car is different and has aged differently. No one can tell you exactly what your car is worth - it will take a bit of negotiating to get the price right.

Find out more in our Car Valuation for Sellers section.
