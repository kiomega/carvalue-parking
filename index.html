Selling your car

Getting the right amount of money when you are selling a car is critical. Value the car too high and no one will buy. Value the car too low and you'll miss out on extra cash.

Carvalue.com.au has an entire section dedicated to selling your used car. Find out how to get your car valued, common mistakes car sellers make, and simple ways to increase your car's value.

Buying a new or used car

Buying a new or used car is an exciting moment in everyone's life. Whether you're upgrading to the latest model Mercedes-Benz, or looking for a cheap zippy, you don't want to pay more than you have to for the car. Research is key.

Visit our section on buying a new or used car to find out how to determine whether you're getting a good deal, common mistakes buyers make, and some simple ways to knock off a few dollars from your next purchase.
